## The SEO Asymmetry

This is not just a cultural bet. It's the only realistic SEO bet available at DR 21.

| | ChatGPT keywords | Claude keywords |
|---|---|---|
| Competition | Saturated — DR 60-80+ sites with years of backlinks | Nearly empty |
| Keyword difficulty | High to very high | Unknown (new) |
| Ranking chance at DR 21 | Near zero | High |
| Search volume trend | Flat or declining | Spiking right now |
| Content quality bar to rank | Extremely high | Low — just be better than SEO farms |

ChatGPT has been the dominant player for 3+ years. Every SEO farm, tech blog, and AI content site has published "how to use ChatGPT for research," "ChatGPT for students," "ChatGPT essay writing" — hundreds of times over. Sites with DR 60-80+ own those SERPs. At DR 21, competing for ChatGPT keywords in the next 6 months is not a viable strategy.

Claude keywords are the opposite: new product, new cultural moment, no established authority, spiking search volume. The window to establish topical authority before higher-DR competitors arrive is measured in weeks, not months.

## The Cluster Architecture

All 15-20 articles cross-link to each other. The hub is the Claude Cowork complete guide — the highest-volume keyword, the most comprehensive piece. Every other article links back to it and links forward to related pieces.

```
Claude Cowork Complete Guide (hub — 1K+/mo)
  ├── Switching from ChatGPT to Claude
  ├── Claude vs ChatGPT for Research
  ├── Getting Started with Claude for Students
  ├── Claude for Literature Reviews → Esy template CTA
  ├── Claude Projects for Research
  ├── Claude Cowork for Students
  └── Claude for Dissertation Research → Esy template CTA
```

Google sees a site with topical authority on "Claude for research and education." The cluster reinforces every individual article's rankings.

## Key Principles

1. **The window is weeks, not months.** The Claude surge is happening now. Articles published in March rank faster and capture more traffic than articles published in May when competition has arrived.
2. **ChatGPT is saturated. Claude is open.** At DR 21, this is not a choice — it's the only realistic SEO bet available.
3. **New users need orientation.** The 60%+ surge in Claude users represents millions of people who need exactly what Esy School provides — workflow guidance for research and writing.
4. **Cluster before expanding.** 20 Claude articles that cross-link build topical authority faster than 5 Claude + 5 ChatGPT + 5 Gemini articles that don't reinforce each other.
5. **Month 3 is the decision point.** Extend if converting. Expand if new opportunities appear. The cluster keeps earning regardless.
