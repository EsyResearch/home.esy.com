## Name

**Esy**  
Pronunciation: **“Eh-see”**

### Etymology
Esy derives from *essay*, from the French *essai* — meaning *an attempt, a trial, a way of thinking something through*.

The name reflects Esy’s core belief:
> Thinking is a process, not a single output.

---

## What Esy Is (Canonical Definition)

**Esy is a workflow-based thinking platform.**

It is designed to help people:
- Research complex topics
- Structure their reasoning
- Build durable intellectual artifacts
- Preserve how conclusions were reached, not just the conclusions themselves

Esy is not a prompt-based text generator.  
Esy is a system for **structured thought**.

---

## What Esy Is NOT

To avoid ambiguity, Esy explicitly rejects the following framings:

- ❌ An “AI essay generator”
- ❌ A shortcut for writing assignments
- ❌ A chat-first productivity tool
- ❌ A content farm or output-maximization system
- ❌ A replacement for human thinking or authorship

AI in Esy is **assistive**, not substitutive.

---

## Core Concept: Workflows

### Definition
In Esy, a **workflow** is a repeatable, inspectable system for thinking.

A workflow defines:
- Steps
- Constraints
- Checks
- Artifacts produced at each stage

Workflows are designed to **externalize cognition** — making thinking visible, reviewable, and improvable.

### Examples (non-exhaustive)
- Research workflows
- Claim verification workflows
- Essay and argument construction workflows
- Visual and scrollytelling workflows
- Learning and concept-mapping workflows

---

## Artifacts

Every Esy workflow produces an **artifact**.

An artifact is:
- Stable (not ephemeral like chat)
- Structured
- Revisitable
- Auditable
- Reusable

Artifacts may include:
- Essays
- Research dossiers
- Visual essays
- Concept maps
- Annotated arguments
- Structured notes with sources

Artifacts are meant to be **read, understood, and trusted**.

---

## Essays in Esy

Essays are an **output**, not the foundation.

Esy treats essays as:
- A form of structured reasoning
- One way (among many) to express thought

Esy does not privilege essays over other forms of knowledge representation.

---

## Research-First Principle

Esy is **research-first by design**.

This means:
- Claims are traced to sources
- Reasoning precedes writing
- Structure precedes fluency
- Uncertainty is allowed and documented

Speed is never prioritized over clarity.

---

## Human-Led, AI-Assisted

Esy operates on a strict principle:

> Humans think. AI assists.

AI in Esy may:
- Help explore ideas
- Assist with structure
- Support revision and synthesis
- Surface gaps or inconsistencies

AI must never:
- Replace authorship
- Hide uncertainty
- Fabricate authority
- Collapse reasoning into output

---

## Editorial Integrity

Esy is built for **credibility**.

This includes:
- Respect for sources
- Transparency of process
- Clear distinction between fact, interpretation, and speculation
- Resistance to engagement-driven distortion

Esy is explicitly anti–content-farm by design.

---

## Who Esy Is For

Esy is for people who care about thinking well:
- Researchers and analysts
- Writers and educators
- Students learning *how* to think, not just what to submit
- Builders documenting complex systems
- Anyone frustrated by disposable AI output

Esy assumes an intellectually serious user.

---

## Long-Term Direction

Esy is built for the long term.

Guiding priorities:
- Durability over novelty
- Systems over hacks
- Clarity over speed
- Trust over virality

Esy aims to become:
> A system of record for structured thought.
