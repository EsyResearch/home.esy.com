## Note on AI-Generated Images

For this **photorealistic** visual essay, AI-generated images should be **avoided** for historical artifacts and figures. Use only:
- Museum photography
- Public domain historical images
- Licensed stock photography
- Corporate press materials

AI may be used only for:
- Diagrams (bottle evolution, market share charts)
- Maps (global distribution, expansion routes)
- Infographics (timeline, statistics)
- Decorative elements (patterns, textures)
