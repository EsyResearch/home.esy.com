# Era Guide: The Complete History of Soda — Visual Treatment by Period

> Research Orchestrator | January 1, 2026

## Overview

This guide defines the visual treatment for each historical era in The Complete History of Soda visual essay. These treatments inform color grading, texture overlays, typography adjustments, and animation styles.

---

## Era 1: Scientific Discovery (1767-1780s)

### Chapters
- Chapter 1: The Scientific Spark

### Historical Context
- Georgian England / Age of Enlightenment
- Scientific experimentation flourishing
- Leeds brewery setting (industrial revolution beginning)
- Royal Society recognition and prestige

### Visual Characteristics

| Element | Treatment |
|---------|-----------|
| **Color Temperature** | Warm (+600K) |
| **Primary Tones** | Aged paper, sepia, candlelight amber |
| **Accent Colors** | Copper (scientific instruments), glass green |
| **Contrast** | Low-medium, soft shadows |
| **Grain** | Heavy (15-20% opacity) |
| **Texture Overlay** | Scientific paper, engraving lines |
| **Vignette** | Strong radial from center |

### Reference Palette
- Background: #1A1612 (aged umber)
- Primary: #8B7355 (old paper)
- Accent: #B87333 (copper)
- Highlight: #C4A77D (candlelight)
- Secondary: #445544 (glass green)

### Typography Adjustments
- Serif fonts with scientific/academic feel
- Period-appropriate letterforms (Caslon, Baskerville)
- Scientific notation styling for chemical formulas
- Engraved title treatments

### Animation Notes
- Slow, deliberate movements
- Bubbles rising through liquid (carbonation visualization)
- Candlelight flicker ambient layer
- Scientific diagram reveal animations

## Era Transition Guidelines

### Transition Timing
- Era shifts occur during scroll-lock sequences
- Color grade shift: 1200ms duration
- Crossfade between era palettes

### Transition Triggers

| From Era | To Era | Trigger Point |
|----------|--------|---------------|
| Scientific → Commercial | End of Ch 1 | Schweppe introduction |
| Commercial → Pharmacy | End of Ch 2 | American soda fountain emergence |
| Pharmacy → Brand Wars | End of Ch 3 | Contour bottle introduction |
| Brand Wars → WWII | End of Ch 4 | Woodruff WWII directive |
| WWII → Cola Wars | End of Ch 5 | Nixon-Khrushchev moment |
| Cola Wars → Global | End of Ch 6 | Post-New Coke recovery |

### Smooth Transition Technique
1. During scroll-lock sequence, begin subtle color shift (20-40% through)
2. Grain/texture gradually reduces or changes
3. By end of scroll-lock, new era treatment is fully applied
4. New chapter opens in complete new era aesthetic
