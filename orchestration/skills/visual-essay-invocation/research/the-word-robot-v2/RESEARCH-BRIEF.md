## Primary Research Questions

### Etymology & Birth (1920s)
1. What is the complete etymology of "robot" from Czech "robota"?
2. What was the cultural/political context of Karel Čapek's R.U.R. (1920)?
3. How did Josef Čapek contribute to the word's creation?
4. What were the original robots in R.U.R. — biological or mechanical?
5. How quickly did the word spread internationally after the play's premiere?

### Imagination & Fiction (1940s-1960s)
6. How did Isaac Asimov's Three Laws (1942) reshape the robot narrative from fear to ethics?
7. When and how was "robotics" first coined as a term?
8. What role did pulp magazines play in robot cultural imagination?
9. How did Metropolis (1927) establish the visual template for robots?

### Industrial Reality (1960s-1980s)
10. When and where was the first industrial robot deployed (Unimate)?
11. Who was Joseph Engelberger and why is he called "father of robotics"?
12. What is Masahiro Mori's "uncanny valley" hypothesis (1970)?
13. How did labor unions and workers respond to industrial automation?

### Fear Peak (1980s-1990s)
14. How did The Terminator (1984) crystallize robot-as-existential-threat?
15. What cultural anxieties did Blade Runner (1982) explore about artificial beings?
16. What was AIBO (1999) and how did it signal a shift toward companion robots?

### Domestication (2000s-2010s)
17. How did Roomba (2002) change public perception of robots?
18. Who is Rodney Brooks and what was his philosophy of "embodied AI"?
19. Who is Cynthia Breazeal and what is social robotics (Kismet, Jibo)?
20. What role did Mars rovers play in making robots into heroes?

### Coexistence & Intimacy (2010s-2025)
21. What is the current state of humanoid robotics (Boston Dynamics, Figure, Tesla Optimus)?
22. What academic research exists on human-robot relationships?
23. Who is David Levy and what did "Love and Sex with Robots" (2007) predict?
24. Who is Kate Devlin and what is her research on intimacy with machines?
25. What are the legal/ethical frameworks emerging for robot relationships (marriage, consent, rights)?
26. What does Hiroshi Ishiguro's Geminoid research reveal about human acceptance of humanoids?
27. What does Sherry Turkle's research warn about preferring robots to humans?
28. What do surveys (Pew, Just Capital, etc.) reveal about public attitudes toward robots and AI?
29. What is the current state of "sex robots" or intimate companion AI?
30. What ethical debates surround robot sexuality (objectification, therapy, consent simulation)?
