# Claim Registry: Why Venezuela Matters

> Last Updated: January 3, 2026

## Purpose

This registry tracks high-stakes factual claims that require verification. Each claim must be verified before inclusion in the essay, or explicitly flagged as uncertain.

**Format per claim:**
- Claim ID
- Claim statement
- Category
- Stakes level
- Status
- Verification targets
- Notes / what would falsify it

## Usage Guidelines

1. **CONFIRMED claims**: Can be stated as fact with citation
2. **DISPUTED claims**: Present both sides; do not take position
3. **REPORTED claims**: Flag as "reported but not independently verified"
4. **UNCLEAR claims**: State uncertainty explicitly; do not present as settled
