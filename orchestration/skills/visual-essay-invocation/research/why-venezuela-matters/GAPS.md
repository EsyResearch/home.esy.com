# Research Gaps: Why Venezuela Matters

> Last Updated: January 3, 2026

## Purpose

This document identifies areas where:
- Research is incomplete

**CRITICAL**: Claims listed here should NOT be stated as fact in the essay. They should be flagged as uncertain, developing, or omitted.

## Developing Facts (January 3, 2026)

### Casualty Figures

**Gap**: Exact number of casualties from Operation Absolute Resolve

- **What we know**: Venezuelan Defense Minister said strikes "hit civilian areas" and officials are "compiling information on the dead and injured"
- **Reported but unverified**: ~40 casualties (military + civilian) per Wikipedia aggregation
- **What we don't know**: Independent verification; breakdown of military vs. civilian; U.S. acknowledgment
- **Risk level**: HIGH — stating specific numbers without verification is dangerous
- **Recommendation**: State "casualties reported but not independently verified" or omit specific numbers

---

### Full Operational Details

**Gap**: Complete list of targets, munitions used, and strike sequence

- **What we know**: Military installations targeted; 150+ aircraft involved; Delta Force led extraction
- **What we don't know**: Full target list; secondary effects; infrastructure damage
- **Risk level**: MEDIUM — military details often classified or contested
- **Recommendation**: Use confirmed operational details only; avoid speculation

---

### Ground Control Reality

**Gap**: Who actually controls Venezuelan institutions, security forces, ports, and PDVSA day-to-day

- **What we know**: VP Rodriguez appeared on TV; Defense Minister said they will resist; PDVSA operating
- **What we don't know**: Whether military is fragmented; whether local officials are cooperating with anyone; port control
- **Risk level**: HIGH — governance reality is completely unclear
- **Recommendation**: Explicitly state "ground control remains uncertain" in essay

---

### "Running Venezuela" Implementation

**Gap**: What Trump's statement about "running" Venezuela means operationally

- **What we know**: Trump said U.S. will "run the country" until transition
- **What we don't know**: Governance structure; who makes decisions; legal framework; personnel
- **Risk level**: HIGH — rhetoric vs. implementation gap
- **Recommendation**: Present as stated claim, not as implemented reality

---

### Congressional Response Outcome

**Gap**: Whether War Powers resolutions will pass; what legal consequences follow

- **What we know**: Resolutions introduced; initial votes failed by narrow margins
- **What we don't know**: Final outcome; judicial challenges; enforcement
- **Risk level**: MEDIUM — developing political story
- **Recommendation**: Report what's introduced; note outcome pending
