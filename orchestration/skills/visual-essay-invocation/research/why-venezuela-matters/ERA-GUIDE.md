# Era Guide: Why Venezuela Matters

> Last Updated: January 3, 2026

## Visual Treatment Philosophy

This is a **systems-first, diagram-driven** visual essay. Unlike biographical or historical essays that rely on portraiture and archival photography, this essay prioritizes:

1. **SVG diagrams** explaining processes (oil chemistry, refinery flows, legal frameworks)
2. **Maps** showing geography, shipping lanes, diplomatic positions
3. **Charts** showing conceptual relationships (not fake data)
4. **Timeline bands** showing historical progression
5. **Restrained photography** where necessary (no sensational imagery)

### Editorial Constraints

- **NO raid/capture imagery** — No violence, triumph, humiliation
- **NO unverified social media visuals** — AI-generated fakes circulating
- **NO sensational treatment** — Calm, authoritative, objective
- **YES systems diagrams** — Flow charts, decision trees, process maps
- **YES maps with data layers** — Toggle between energy and diplomacy views
- **YES conceptual charts** — Use qualitative bands where precise data unavailable

## Visual Treatment by Topic Area

### 1. Geography and Hemispheric Position

**Treatment**: Clean SVG map with layered information

**Color approach**:
- Ocean: Deep navy (#1a365d)
- Venezuela highlight: Warm gold/amber (#d69e2e)
- U.S. highlight: Slate blue (#4a5568)
- Shipping lanes: Dotted white lines
- Neutral countries: Light gray (#e2e8f0)

**Interactions**:
- Hover reveals country names and brief context
- Toggle between "Energy" layer (oil flows) and "Diplomacy" layer (reaction positions)

**Typography**:
- Country labels: Sans-serif, all caps, subtle
- Region labels: Light weight, minimal

### 2. Oil Chemistry and Refinery Systems

**Treatment**: Technical diagrams with educational clarity

**Color approach**:
- Light crude: Golden yellow (#ecc94b)
- Heavy crude: Deep brown/black (#2d3748)
- Refinery units: Industrial gray with accent borders
- Output products: Color-coded by type (diesel blue, gasoline amber, etc.)

**Diagram types**:
- API gravity scale (vertical thermometer-style)
- Refinery flow diagram (left-to-right process)
- Input/output comparison (split view)

**Interactions**:
- Hover on refinery units reveals function definitions
- Toggle between "light crude path" and "heavy crude path"

**Typography**:
- Technical labels: Monospace or condensed sans
- Definitions: Readable body font in tooltips

### 3. Political Timeline

**Treatment**: Horizontal timeline band with era markers

**Color approach**:
- Pre-Chavez era: Muted sepia (#b7791f)
- Chavez era: Revolutionary red (#c53030)
- Maduro era: Darker red transitioning to crisis amber
- 2026 operation: Alert red marker (#e53e3e)

**Era treatments**:
- 1999-2013 (Chavez): Bold, populist energy
- 2013-2025 (Maduro): Gradual desaturation, decline
- 2026 (Operation): Sharp contrast, uncertainty markers

**Interactions**:
- Hover reveals event details
- Verification flags (⚑) on disputed dates

## Typography System

### Headlines
- **Family**: Strong sans-serif (e.g., Inter, Source Sans Pro)
- **Weight**: Bold (700)
- **Character**: Authoritative, clear

### Body Text
- **Family**: Readable serif or sans-serif
- **Weight**: Regular (400)
- **Line height**: Generous for readability

### Technical/Data Labels
- **Family**: Monospace (e.g., JetBrains Mono, Fira Code)
- **Weight**: Regular
- **Use**: Diagram labels, data points

### Quotes/Callouts
- **Family**: Serif italic
- **Treatment**: Larger size, indented, with attribution

### Captions
- **Family**: Sans-serif
- **Weight**: Light
- **Size**: Smaller than body

## Animation Principles

### Scroll-Lock Sequences

This essay uses **minimal scroll-lock** due to systems/explanatory nature. Where used:

1. **Map exploration** — Scroll reveals layers progressively
2. **Diagram building** — Scroll constructs flow chart step by step
3. **Timeline progression** — Scroll advances through eras

### Timing
- Transitions: 300-500ms
- Easing: Ease-out for reveals, ease-in-out for toggles
- No jarring snaps

### Interaction States
- Hover: Subtle highlight + tooltip
- Active: Slightly depressed appearance
- Focus: Visible outline for accessibility

---

## Accessibility Requirements

1. **Color contrast**: All text meets WCAG AA (4.5:1 minimum)
2. **Alt text**: Every diagram has descriptive alt text
3. **Reduced motion**: Fallback static versions for all animations
4. **Keyboard navigation**: All interactive elements focusable
5. **Screen reader support**: Semantic HTML, ARIA labels
6. **No information in color alone**: Shape/pattern redundancy

## Photo Usage (Limited)

Where photography is necessary:

### Acceptable Subjects
- Maps and archival documents
- Official government buildings (neutral)
- Oil infrastructure (industrial, not sensational)
- Formal portraits of key figures (respectful)

### Prohibited Subjects
- Raid/capture footage
- Violence or casualties
- Humiliation imagery
- Unverified social media content
- AI-generated fakes

### Treatment
- Neutral color grading (no dramatic filters)
- Documentary rather than editorial style
- Clear attribution visible
