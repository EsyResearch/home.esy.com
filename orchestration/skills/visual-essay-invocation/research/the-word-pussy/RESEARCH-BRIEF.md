# Research Brief: The Origin of the Word "Pussy"

## Project Identification

- **Working Title**: Words Have Histories: The Curious Journey of "Pussy"
- **Subtitle**: From Felines to Taboo—How One Word Accumulated a Lifetime of Meanings
- **Topic**: Etymology and semantic evolution of the English word "pussy"
- **Date Initiated**: December 14, 2025
- **Visual Treatment**: Photo-Documentary + Typography-Forward

---

## Research Targets

### Primary Research Questions

1. What are the earliest documented uses of "pussy" and its cognates?
2. How did a word for cats become associated with female anatomy?
3. When and how did the "cowardice" meaning emerge?
4. What linguistic mechanisms (metaphor, euphemism, taboo) drove these shifts?
5. How have dictionaries and lexicographers handled this word across eras?

### Secondary Research Questions

1. What role did the "puss in boots" folklore play in spreading the word?
2. How does "pussy willow" connect to the etymology?
3. What cross-linguistic cognates exist (Dutch, German, Norse)?
4. How does this compare to other words that acquired taboo meanings?

---

## Research Depth

✅ **Deep (15-25 sources for full visual essay)**

This topic requires scholarly etymological sources, historical dictionary entries, and linguistic analysis of semantic change.

## Visual Treatment Philosophy

### Typography as Hero Visual

This essay uses **evolving typefaces** as the primary visual narrative device:
- Each era's meaning reflected in period-appropriate type
- Blackletter → Humanist → Georgian → Victorian → Modern
- Letterforms tell the story alongside the words

### Documentary Authenticity
- Historical dictionary pages and manuscript facsimiles
- No illustrations—photographic and typographic authenticity
- Museum-quality presentation of linguistic artifacts
