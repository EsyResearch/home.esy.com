---

## Research Scope

### Core Questions to Answer

1. **Etymology**: Where does the word "Han" (漢) originate? What is its connection to the Han River (漢水)?
2. **Dynastic Legacy**: How did the Han Dynasty (206 BCE–220 CE) become so foundational that "Han" became synonymous with Chinese civilization?
3. **Linguistic Diffusion**: How did the word travel to Korea, Japan, and Vietnam?
4. **Script Systems**: How did "Han characters" become the common term (Hanja 한자/漢字, Kanji 漢字, Hán tự 漢字)?
5. **Identity Formation**: When and how did "Han Chinese" (漢族) become an ethnic identifier?
6. **Korean Recontextualization**: How did Korea use 韓 (a different "Han") in Hanguk (韓國) and Hangul (한글)?
7. **Modern Resonance**: What role does "Han" play in contemporary East Asian identity and geopolitics?

### Time Scope

- **Primary focus**: 206 BCE (Han Dynasty founding) to present
- **Deep coverage**: Han Dynasty period, spread to Korea/Japan/Vietnam, modern nation-building
- **Background**: Pre-Han origins of the character and Han River

### Geographic Scope

- China (primary origin)
- Korea (significant recontextualization)
- Japan (script adoption)
- Vietnam (historical usage and departure)
