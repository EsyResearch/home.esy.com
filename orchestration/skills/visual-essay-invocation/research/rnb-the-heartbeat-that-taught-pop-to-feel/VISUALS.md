## Primary Archive Sources

**URL**: https://www.gettyimages.com/

- Michael Ochs Archives collection
- Performance and studio shots
**Key Collections**:
- Michael Ochs Archives (definitive R&B collection)
- Hulton Archive (British perspective)
- Premium Archive (editorial)

## Licensing Notes

### Editorial Use Considerations
- Artist photographs generally require editorial license
- Album artwork may require separate permissions
- Documentary stills may have film studio rights
- Studio interiors may require owner permission

### Rights Clearance Priority
1. Wikimedia Commons CC-licensed (easiest)
2. Institutional open access (usually free)
3. Getty editorial license (standard cost)
4. Private archives (negotiated)
5. Estate permissions (case by case)

### Budget Allocation Recommendation
- 40% Getty/stock editorial licenses
- 30% Open access/CC sources
- 20% Institutional partnerships
- 10% Custom/estate permissions
