## Project Overview

A deeply researched, photo-driven visual essay tracing the roots, origins, inspirations, derivatives, and evolution of R&B (Rhythm & Blues) — from its postwar naming and Black popular music ecosystems to its constant reinvention across Soul, Funk, Disco adjacency, Quiet Storm, New Jack Swing, 90s Contemporary R&B, Neo-Soul, and today's global hybrid forms.

## Visual Treatment

- **Medium**: Photorealistic (archival photography)
- **Hero**: Non-scroll-locked (explicit requirement)
- **Eras**: Multiple distinct visual treatments spanning 1940s–present
- **Design System**: Original, R&B-derived (no copied patterns)
