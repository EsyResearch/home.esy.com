## Further Reading Recommendations

1. **Čapek, Karel.** *R.U.R. (Rossum's Universal Robots)*. Translated by Claudia Novack. Penguin Classics, 2004. — The original play that introduced "robot"

2. **Asimov, Isaac.** *I, Robot*. Gnome Press, 1950. — Shaped public understanding of robot ethics

3. **Wilson, Daniel H.** *Where's My Jetpack?: A Guide to the Amazing Science Fiction Future that Never Arrived*. Bloomsbury, 2007. — Cultural expectations vs. reality

4. **Mori, Masahiro.** "The Uncanny Valley." *IEEE Robotics & Automation Magazine*, 2012 (English translation). — Seminal psychology of robot acceptance

5. **Čapek, Karel.** *The War with the Newts*. 1936. — Related Čapek work exploring similar themes
