## Photography Style Guide

### Light to Shadow Progression

| Act | Light Quality | Color Temperature | Mood |
|-----|---------------|-------------------|------|
| I (Creators) | Bright, golden | Warm (3000-4000K) | Wonder, majesty |
| II (Order) | Clear, balanced | Neutral (5000K) | Structure, meaning |
| III (Tricksters) | Dappled, shifting | Mixed | Playful unease |
| IV (Threshold) | Dim, amber | Cool-warm tension | Growing dread |
| V (Terror) | Dark, isolated | Cool (6000K+), desaturated | Horror |

### Visual Processing by Era/Tradition

| Tradition | Processing | Notes |
|-----------|------------|-------|
| Yoruba | Rich, saturated | Celebrate color and life |
| Egyptian | Golden, ancient | Timeless, monumental |
| Akan | Gold leaf, warm | Royal, precious |
| Zulu | Earth tones, natural | Grounded, organic |
| Fon/Dahomey | Deep reds, brass | Blood and metal |
| Terror entities | Desaturated, cold | Drain of life and warmth |
