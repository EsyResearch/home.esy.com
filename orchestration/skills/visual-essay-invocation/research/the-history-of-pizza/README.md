## Research Scope

This visual essay traces pizza's complete history from:
- Ancient flatbread predecessors (10,000 BCE - 500 CE)
- Medieval and Renaissance developments (500 - 1700 CE)
- Birth of modern pizza in Naples (1700s - 1889)
- The Margherita legend and verification
- Immigration and American transformation (1900 - 1945)
- Post-WWII explosion and franchising era
- Artisanal revival and global phenomenon (1990s - present)

## Etymology Deep-Dive

Comprehensive tracing of the word "pizza" from:
- Byzantine Greek πίτα (pita)
- Latin pinsa/pinsere (to pound/stamp)
- Old High German bizzo (morsel/bite)
- First documented use: Gaeta, 997 CE
- Evolution through Italian dialects
