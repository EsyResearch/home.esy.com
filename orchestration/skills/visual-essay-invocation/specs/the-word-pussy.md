# Visual Essay Invocation: Words Have Histories

## The Curious Journey of "Pussy"

---

## Layer 1: Strategic Foundation

### Project Title

**Words Have Histories: The Curious Journey of "Pussy"**

*A visual essay exploring how a simple pet-name for cats became one of the most controversial words in English—through the mechanisms of affection, metaphor, and taboo*

### Executive Brief

**Emotional Throughline**: Fascination → Understanding → Appreciation for linguistic complexity

**Stakes**: Language shapes how we think. Understanding why a word became taboo reveals how culture shapes vocabulary—and how vocabulary, in turn, reinforces culture.

**Transformation Promise**: The reader will never think about word origins the same way. They'll understand the euphemism treadmill, semantic drift, and how the most "forbidden" words often have the most fascinating histories.

**Central Question**: How did a word for cats become one of the most avoided words in polite English?

### Visual Treatment Philosophy

**Typography-Forward Documentary**

This visual essay breaks from the photography-centered norm. **Typography IS the visual**. The word "pussy" rendered in period-appropriate typefaces is the hero image. Letterforms tell the story of time passing.

**Rules**:
- The word itself, in evolving typefaces, is the primary visual element
- Historical dictionary pages and manuscript facsimiles as supporting visuals
- No modern photography of cats or people
- No illustrations—photographic and typographic authenticity only
- Each era has a distinct typographic identity
- Minimal but strategic use of historical imagery

**Why This Works**:
Etymology is about *words*. The most authentic visual representation of a word is... the word itself. Typography specimens are the artifacts of language history.
