## Sensitive Content Guidelines

### Atrocity Photography
- **Congo hands**: Use sparingly; provide context; content warning
- **Genocide imagery**: Essential for accuracy; handle with care
- **Violence**: Focus on systemic documentation, not gratuitous display

### Contextual Framing
- Pair European portraits with African victims/leaders
- Show before/after (pre-colonial societies vs. colonial impact)
- Emphasize agency alongside victimization
