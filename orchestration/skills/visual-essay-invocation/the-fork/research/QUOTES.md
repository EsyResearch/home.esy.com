## Quote Usage Guidelines

### For Writers

When using these quotes in the visual essay:

1. **Verified (‚úÖ)**: Use freely with attribution
2. **Attributed (‚ö†Ô∏è)**: Use with attribution; note "attributed to" if being precise
3. **Paraphrased (üìù)**: Do not present as direct quote; describe as "accounts suggest" or "chroniclers noted"

### Quote Integration Examples

**Good** (for attributed quote):
> Peter Damian, the fierce reforming cardinal, delivered his verdict: the princess's fork use was "excessive delicacy."

**Better** (acknowledging attribution):
> In his writings, Peter Damian reportedly condemned the princess's fork, describing how "she would impale [food] on a certain golden instrument with two prongs."

**Avoid** (presenting paraphrase as quote):
> ‚ùå Catherine de' Medici said "I eat in the Italian manner."
