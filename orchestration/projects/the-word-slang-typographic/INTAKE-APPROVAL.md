## Visual Language Constraints

**ALLOWED:**
- Typographic compositions
- Abstract vector illustrations
- Geometric shapes and patterns
- UI-like components (cards, tags, chips)
- Recreated print textures as vector/pattern
- Maps/diagrams as line art

**NOT ALLOWED:**
- Photographs
- "Found" images framed as photos
- Photorealistic portraits

## Research Standards

1. No invented sources, dates, "first uses," or quotations
2. Every historical claim must be cited
3. Uncertain etymology must be labeled with competing hypotheses
4. Respectful treatment of speech communities
5. Typography choices justified by content, not vibes
