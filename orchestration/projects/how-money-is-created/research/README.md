## Key Findings

### Core Thesis (Strongly Supported)
**"In modern economies, money is created primarily through credit, not printing presses."**

**Evidence**: Bank of England (2014), Werner (2014), ECB (2014), Federal Reserve publications - **HIGH CONFIDENCE**

### Key Mechanisms (Strongly Supported)
1. **Loans create deposits** (not the reverse) - **HIGH CONFIDENCE**
2. **Commercial banks create most money** through lending - **HIGH CONFIDENCE**
3. **Central banks influence but don't directly create most money** - **HIGH CONFIDENCE**
4. **Money is destroyed through loan repayment** - **HIGH CONFIDENCE**
5. **Capital requirements constrain money creation** - **HIGH CONFIDENCE**

### Constraints (Strongly Supported)
- Capital requirements (regulatory)
- Risk assessment (confidence)
- Demand for loans (market forces)
- Interest rates (policy + market)

**All constraints confirmed with HIGH confidence.**
