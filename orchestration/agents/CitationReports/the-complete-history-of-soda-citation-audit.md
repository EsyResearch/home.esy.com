## Issues Identified

### Important Issues

#### Issue 1: Pepsi Creation Date Discrepancy
- **Category**: Claim-Citation
- **Location**: Era 3 / Pharmacy Counter section
- **Description**: Essay states "Caleb Bradham created Pepsi-Cola in 1893" but NCpedia indicates the beverage was created "in the late 1890s" with the name "Pepsi-Cola" adopted in 1898 and trademark registered in 1902-1903.
- **Evidence**: NCpedia states: "In the late 1890s he produced a beverage of his own creation... In 1898, however, for reasons no one knows, young Bradham changed the name to 'Pepsi-Cola.'"
- **Recommended Fix**: Change to "Caleb Bradham created Brad's Drink in 1893 (renamed Pepsi-Cola in 1898)" OR align with scholarly consensus on the 1893 date if alternate sources support it.
- **Severity**: Minor (the 1893 date is widely cited, including by Pharmacy Times)
