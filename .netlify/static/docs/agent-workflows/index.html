What Are Agent Workflows?

Traditional AI chat is one question, one answer. Agent workflows are different: they're sequences of AI actions that work together to complete complex tasks. Think of it like having a research assistant who can:

  • Read and remember your documents
  • Break big tasks into smaller steps
  • Pass information between steps automatically
  • Generate structured, citation-backed output

The ‚Äúagentic‚Äù part means the AI takes initiative‚Äîit doesn't just respond to prompts, it plans and executes research strategies based on your goals.
