## Tier 1: Primary Sources & Institutional Archives

### C1. British Library — Diamond Sutra
- **Asset**: Diamond Sutra (Or.8210/P.2)
- **Date**: 868 CE
- **Significance**: Oldest dated complete printed book
- **URL**: https://www.bl.uk/collection-items/the-diamond-sutra
- **Tier**: 1 (Primary source artifact)

### C2. Library of Congress — Gutenberg Bible
- **Collection**: Gutenberg Bible and Early Printing
- **URL**: https://guides.loc.gov/gutenberg
- **Tier**: 1 (Institutional primary collection)

### C3. Gutenberg Museum, Mainz
- **Collection**: Gutenberg Bible, press reconstructions, printing artifacts
- **URL**: https://www.mainz.de/microsite/gutenberg-museum-en/
- **Tier**: 1 (Institutional primary collection)

### C4. UNESCO — Jikji
- **Document**: Memory of the World Register entry
- **Date inscribed**: September 2001
- **Significance**: Confirmed as oldest surviving metal movable type book
- **URL**: https://en.unesco.org/memoryoftheworld/
- **Tier**: 1 (International institutional verification)

### C5. Bibliothèque nationale de France — Jikji
- **Asset**: Jikji (second volume)
- **Date**: 1377
- **Location**: Manuscrits Orientaux division
- **Tier**: 1 (Primary source artifact)

### C6. Göttingen State and University Library — Helmasperger Instrument
- **Document**: Legal record of Fust vs. Gutenberg lawsuit
- **Date**: November 6, 1455
- **Significance**: Only contemporary account of Gutenberg's business and invention
- **Tier**: 1 (Primary source document)

### C7. Shen Kuo — *Dream Pool Essays* (*Mengxi Bitan*)
- **Date**: c. 1088
- **Significance**: Primary source for Bi Sheng's movable type invention
- **Translation**: Thomas Carter, *The Invention of Printing in China* (1955)
- **Tier**: 1 (Primary historical source)

### C8. Wang Zhen — *Nong Shu* (Book of Agriculture)
- **Date**: 1313
- **Significance**: Documents wooden movable type and revolving typecase
- **Tier**: 1 (Primary historical source)
