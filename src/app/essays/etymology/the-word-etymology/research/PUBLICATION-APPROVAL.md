## Content Summary

### Narrative Arc

1. **Hook**: Etymology is the biography of words, and this is the biography of "etymology" itself
2. **Greek Origins**: Plato's Cratylus, étymon ("true sense") + logía ("study of")
3. **Roman Translation**: Cicero's veriloquium, Varro's De Lingua Latina
4. **Medieval Preservation**: Isidore of Seville's Etymologiae
5. **English Arrival**: First attestation c. 1350-1400
6. **Semantic Shift**: From "true meaning" to "historical study"
7. **Scientific Revolution**: Jones, Grimm, Bopp establish comparative linguistics
8. **Dictionary Era**: Johnson, Murray, Webster codify etymological method
9. **Digital Age**: Etymonline democratizes access
10. **Invitation**: Every word has a story — etymology is how we find it
