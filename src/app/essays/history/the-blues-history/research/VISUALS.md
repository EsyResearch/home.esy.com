## NOTES

1. **Pre-1928 US Publications:** All photographs published in the United States before January 1, 1928 are in the public domain.

2. **Carl Van Vechten Collection:** Major source for 1930s-40s blues portraits; Van Vechten estate donated to Library of Congress.

3. **Lomax Collection:** 400+ photographs from 1934-1950 field recordings; primary source for Delta blues documentation.

4. **Mississippi Blues Trail:** 200+ historical markers throughout Mississippi; marker imagery often CC-licensed.

5. **Copyright Caution:** Robert Johnson photographs remain in legal dispute; use contextual imagery or licensed stock.
