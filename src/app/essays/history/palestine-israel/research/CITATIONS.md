## ✅ Fixed Issues (December 23, 2024)

### Issue 1: Rabin Quote - FIXED ✅

**Original (Unverified)**:
> "We did not take any action to preserve the honor and lives of the Arabs..."

**Fixed Version**:
> "'Driving out' is a term with a harsh ring. Psychologically, this was one of the most difficult actions we undertook. The population of Lod did not leave willingly."
> — Yitzhak Rabin (From his memoirs, passage censored in 1979 and later restored)

**Source**: Rabin's memoirs, leaked to NYT by Peretz Kidron, October 23, 1979

---

### Issue 2: Raymond of Aguilers Quote - FIXED ✅

**Original (Conflated)**:
> "The massacre was terrible; the weights of the feet of our horses..."

**Fixed Version**:
> "In the Temple and porch of Solomon, men rode in blood up to their knees and bridle reins."
> — Raymond of Aguilers (Crusader chronicler, imagery drawn from Revelation 14:20)

**Source**: Raymond of Aguilers' chronicle of the First Crusade

---

### Issue 3: Ben-Gurion Quote - FIXED ✅

**Original (Word Order Reversed)**:
> "We will fight the White Paper as if there were no war..."

**Fixed Version**:
> "We will fight the war as if there were no White Paper, and we will fight the White Paper as if there were no war."
> — David Ben-Gurion, 1939

---

### Issue 4: Josephus Quote - FIXED ✅

**Original (Paraphrase)**:
> "Jerusalem was so thoroughly razed to the ground..."

**Fixed Version**:
> "All the rest of the wall encompassing the city was so completely levelled to the ground as to leave future visitors to the spot no ground for believing that it had ever been inhabited."
> — Josephus, The Jewish War, Book VII (75 CE)
