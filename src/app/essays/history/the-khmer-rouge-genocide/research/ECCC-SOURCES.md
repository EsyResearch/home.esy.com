# ECCC Tribunal Sources: Research Citation Report

## 1. ECCC Case 001 - Kaing Guek Eav (Duch)

**Trial Chamber Judgment Date**: July 26, 2010
**Appeal Judgment Date**: February 3, 2012

**Official URLs**:
- ECCC Defendant Profile: https://www.eccc.gov.kh/en/cases/charged-profile/kaing-guek-eav
- Appeal Judgment PDF: https://www.eccc.gov.kh/sites/default/files/documents/courtdoc/Case%20001AppealJudgementEn.pdf
- **Source Tier**: Tier 1

**Key Findings**:
- **Defendant**: Kaing Guek Eav (alias "Duch"), Chairman of S-21 Security Centre
- **Convictions**: Crimes against humanity, grave breaches of Geneva Conventions
- **Victims**: At least 12,272 victims executed at S-21
- **Final Sentence**: Life imprisonment (Supreme Court Chamber, 2012)
- **Death**: September 2, 2020, at age 77

## 2. ECCC Case 002/01 - First Trial

**Trial Chamber Judgment Date**: August 7, 2014
**Appeal Judgment Date**: November 23, 2016

**Official URLs**:
- Trial Overview: https://www.eccc.gov.kh/en/cases/case-002/trial-01
- **Source Tier**: Tier 1

**Key Findings**:
- **Defendants**: Nuon Chea ("Brother Number Two"), Khieu Samphan (Head of State)
- **Scope**: Forced population transfers (April 1975 - December 1977)
- **Convictions**: Crimes against humanity (murder, political persecution, other inhumane acts)
- **Sentence**: Life imprisonment for both
- **Trial Statistics**: 222 hearing days; 92 witnesses; 103,274 public attendees

---

## 3. ECCC Case 002/02 - Genocide Conviction

**Trial Chamber Judgment Date**: November 16, 2018
**Appeal Judgment Date**: September 22, 2022

**Official URLs**:
- Trial Overview: https://www.eccc.gov.kh/en/cases/case-002/trial-02
- UN Legal Office Summary: https://legal.un.org/ola/media/info_from_lc/mss/speeches/MSS-Phnom-Penh-November-2018-ECCC-2-2-summary-of-judgement.pdf
- **Source Tier**: Tier 1

**Genocide Convictions**:
- **Nuon Chea**: Guilty of genocide against ethnic Vietnamese AND Cham Muslims
- **Khieu Samphan**: Guilty of genocide against ethnic Vietnamese

**Historical Significance**:
This was the **first genocide conviction rendered by a hybrid criminal tribunal**.

## 5. ECCC Residual Functions (Post-2023)

**Commencement Date**: January 1, 2023
**Current Status**: Active residual phase

**Key Functions**:
1. Archive Management and preservation
2. Ongoing declassification of judicial records
3. Reparations monitoring
4. Victim support initiatives
5. Educational outreach ("Extraordinary Bus" program)

## Death Toll Statistics (from ECCC)

- **Consensus estimate**: 1.7-2 million deaths
- **Mass graves documented**: 23,745 sites (1.3 million suspected execution victims)
- **Vietnamese victims**: "Almost all Vietnamese people remaining in Cambodia were killed"
- **Cham Muslim victims**: 90,000-500,000 killed
