## PRE-K-POP ERA

### On Cho Yong-pil's Influence
> "Just as music in the Western world can be divided into before and after the Beatles, Korean music can be divided into before and after Cho."
> — **Im Jin-mo**, Music Critic

### On Nam Jin/Na Hoon-a Rivalry
> "The 'show planners,' who organized concerts, worked with journalists to create the rivalry between us to attract public interest. It was all manufactured by the times."
> — **Nam Jin**

### On Yoo Jae-ha's Influence
> "I went to music school because Yoo Jae-ha went to music school."
> — **You Hee-yeol**

### On Han Dae-soo and Protest
> "The dictator's sole fear was the students, and the students loved Hahn Dae-soo."
> — Historical account

### On Korean Trot's Emotional Core
> "The combination of han and heung is what defines ppong, and this definitive combination, which characterizes trot, is also at the heart of K-pop."
> — Academic analysis
