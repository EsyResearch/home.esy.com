## ERA SKINS (Chronological)

### 1. PRE-K-POP ROOTS (1926-1991)
**Visual Identity**: Sepia & Muted Earth Tones

| Element | Specification |
|---------|---------------|
| **Color Palette** | Sepia browns, warm cream, faded gold, military olive |
| **Typography** | Traditional Korean brush influence; vintage Korean typefaces |
| **Textures** | Aged paper, vinyl record grooves, radio static grain |
| **Imagery** | Black & white photographs, US military base signage, protest imagery |
| **Motion** | Slow fades, film grain overlays, vintage broadcast effects |
| **Audio Cues** | Trot melodies, folk guitar, censorship "beeps" |

**Key Visual Motifs**:
- 8th Army Show stages
- Early Korean TV broadcasts
- Democracy protest crowds
- Vinyl records and radio dials

## TRANSITION GUIDELINES

### Era-to-Era Transitions
- Use **crossfade** for smooth chronological progression
- Include **date stamp** animations at era boundaries
- Layer **audio transitions** (fade out old era, fade in new)

### Concept Skin Activations
- Concept skins **overlay** era skins, not replace
- Use **subtle color temperature shifts** when entering concept territory
- Maintain era authenticity while adding thematic layer

### Mobile Considerations
- Ensure all gradients work on limited color depth
- Test motion effects at 30fps for older devices
- Provide static fallbacks for complex animations
