## Source Quality Tiers

### Tier 1 (Primary - Prioritized)
- Academic books and peer-reviewed papers
- Korean government/industry official statistics
- Documented interviews with artists and industry figures
- Billboard, Gaon/Circle chart official data
- Museum and archive collections

### Tier 2 (High-Quality Secondary)
- Major journalism outlets (Korean and international)
- Industry trade publications
- Reputable documentaries with primary sources
- Verified quotes and statements

### Tier 3 (Use with Caution)
- Wikipedia (as gateway to primary sources only)
- Fan-compiled databases (verify independently)
- Social media posts (only from verified accounts)

### Tier 4 (Avoided)
- Tabloid journalism
- Unverified fan rumors
- Content farms and aggregators
