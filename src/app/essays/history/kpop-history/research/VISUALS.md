## LICENSING PRIORITIES

### Tier 1: Free/Open Sources
1. **Wikimedia Commons**: Public domain historical images
2. **Official press kits**: When artists/agencies provide
3. **US Government archives**: Military, diplomatic imagery
4. **UN archives**: Public speeches, events
5. **Creative Commons**: Properly attributed content

### Tier 2: Stock Photography
1. **Getty Images**: Editorial K-pop coverage
2. **Shutterstock**: Industry/location imagery
3. **AP Images**: News event coverage
4. **Reuters**: International news coverage

### Tier 3: Direct Licensing
1. **Agency archives**: SM, YG, JYP, HYBE
2. **Broadcaster archives**: KBS, MBC, SBS, Mnet
3. **Concert photography**: Specific event rights
4. **Documentary stills**: Film/TV production companies

## RIGHTS CONSIDERATIONS

### Fair Use Potential (US Context)
- Critical commentary on industry practices
- Historical documentation
- Transformative educational use
- Limited portions for illustration

### Rights Clearance Required
- Full album artwork reproduction
- Extended video clips
- Commercial promotional imagery
- Recent official photos

### Public Domain Confirmation
- Pre-1928 imagery (US)
- Korean government works
- US government works
- Works with expired copyright
