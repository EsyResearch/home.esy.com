## FOUNDATIONAL GENOME PAPERS

### Neanderthal Genome

**Green et al. 2010 - Draft Neanderthal Genome**
- Green RE, Krause J, Briggs AW, et al. "A Draft Sequence of the Neandertal Genome." *Science* 328(5979):710-722 (2010)
- DOI: 10.1126/science.1188021
- Institution: Max Planck Institute for Evolutionary Anthropology
- Significance: First draft sequence demonstrating gene flow from Neanderthals into non-African ancestors

**Prüfer et al. 2014 - High-Coverage Neanderthal Genome**
- Prüfer K, Racimo F, Patterson N, et al. "The complete genome sequence of a Neanderthal from the Altai Mountains." *Nature* 505:43-49 (2014)
- DOI: 10.1038/nature12886
- Significance: First high-quality Neanderthal genome; revealed inbreeding and multiple gene flow events

### Denisovan Genome

**Meyer et al. 2012 - High-Coverage Denisovan Genome**
- Meyer M, Kircher M, Gansauge MT, et al. "A High-Coverage Genome Sequence from an Archaic Denisovan Individual." *Science* 338(6104):222-226 (2012)
- DOI: 10.1126/science.1224344
- Significance: 30x coverage enabling detailed population genetics analysis

**Slon et al. 2018 - Neanderthal-Denisovan Hybrid**
- Slon V, et al. "The genome of the offspring of a Neanderthal mother and a Denisovan father." *Nature* 561:113-116 (2018)
- DOI: 10.1038/s41586-018-0455-x
- Significance: First direct evidence of Neanderthal-Denisovan hybridization

### 2024 Admixture Timing

**Nature 2024 - Precise Admixture Dating**
- "Gene flow occurred approximately 50,500-43,500 years ago"
- DOI: 10.1038/s41586-024-08420-x

**Science 2024 - Admixture Duration**
- "Interbreeding lasted approximately 7,000 years"
- DOI: 10.1126/science.adi1768

## MAJOR FOSSIL DISCOVERY PAPERS

### Early Homo sapiens

**Hublin et al. 2017 - Jebel Irhoud**
- Hublin J-J, et al. "New fossils from Jebel Irhoud, Morocco and the pan-African origin of Homo sapiens." *Nature* 546:289-292 (2017)
- DOI: 10.1038/nature22336
- Significance: Oldest securely dated H. sapiens (315,000 ± 34,000 years)

**Richter et al. 2017 - Jebel Irhoud Dating**
- Richter D, et al. "The age of the hominin fossils from Jebel Irhoud, Morocco, and the origins of the Middle Stone Age." *Nature* 546:293-296 (2017)
- DOI: 10.1038/nature22335

**Vidal et al. 2022 - Omo Kibish Revised Dating**
- Vidal CM, et al. "Age of the oldest known Homo sapiens from eastern Africa." *Nature* 601:579-583 (2022)

- DOI: 10.1038/nature01669
