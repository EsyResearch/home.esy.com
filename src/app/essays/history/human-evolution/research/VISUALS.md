## RECONSTRUCTION LABELING REQUIREMENTS

All reconstructions MUST include visible labels:
- "Artist's reconstruction"
- "Scientific reconstruction"
- "AI-assisted reconstruction" (if applicable)

Captions must state:
- "Based on published fossil evidence"
- "Not a photograph"
- Avoid claiming exact features (skin/hair color) unless evidence supports

## SOURCES NOT TO USE

- Academic journal PDF figures (copyrighted)
- Documentary screenshots
- Pinterest/Instagram images
- Stock photos without explicit human subject releases
- "Google Images" without verified licenses
