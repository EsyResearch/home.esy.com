## TIMELINE VISUALIZATION NOTES

### Recommended Scales

**Full Deep Time (4.5 Ga - present)**
- Logarithmic scale recommended
- Key markers: Life, Cambrian, K-Pg, Primates, Hominins

**Hominin Evolution (7 Ma - present)**
- Linear scale possible
- Show multiple coexisting species

**Recent Evolution (500 ka - present)**
- Linear scale
- Show overlap of H. sapiens with other humans
- Admixture events

**Cultural Evolution (100 ka - present)**
- Linear scale
- Tools, symbolism, migration, agriculture

### Uncertainty Visualization

- Use gradient bands for date ranges
- Solid for well-dated, faded for uncertain
- Question marks for disputed dates/attributions

---

*All dates should be presented with uncertainty ranges where known. Avoid false precision.*
