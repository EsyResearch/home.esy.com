## Visual Philosophy

### Era-Based Treatment Guidelines

| Era | Treatment | Source Priority |
|-----|-----------|-----------------|
| **Pre-1973 (Precursors)** | Heavy grain, B&W or faded color, documentary feel | Getty Historical, Smithsonian, LOC |
| **1973–1979 (Birth)** | Gritty, raw, low-light party photography, period color casts | Joe Conzo Jr., Cornell Collection, Getty |
| **1980–1984 (Wild Style)** | Subway era color, graffiti-dominant, urban documentary | Cooper/Chalfant archives, film stills |
| **1985–1993 (Golden Age)** | Crisp color, promotional shots, concert photography | Getty Entertainment, album art |
| **1994–1999 (Bicoastal)** | High production value, magazine quality | The Source/Vibe archives, Getty |
| **2000–Present (Digital)** | HD quality, contemporary documentation | Getty, Wireimage, official sources |

### Caption Format Standard

```
[Subject Name/Description]
[Location], [Year]
[Credit/Photographer]
[Historical Significance: Why this image matters]
```
