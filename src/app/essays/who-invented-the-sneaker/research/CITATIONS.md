## Key Facts Supported

- 1839: Charles Goodyear discovers vulcanization of rubber
- 1917: Converse introduces All Star basketball shoe
- 1920s: Chuck Taylor joins Converse, becomes first athlete endorser
- 1936: Jesse Owens wins 4 gold medals in Berlin wearing Adi Dassler shoes
- 1972: Bill Bowerman creates waffle sole using waffle iron; Nike founded
- 1985: Nike signs Michael Jordan; NBA bans Air Jordans; Nike pays $5,000 per game fines
- 1986: Run-DMC releases "My Adidas," signs $1M endorsement deal (first non-athlete sneaker endorsement)
- 2023: Six pairs of Jordan championship sneakers sell for $8M at Sotheby's
- Current market: $75B+ global sneaker market
- 185 years of sneaker evolution (1839-2024)
