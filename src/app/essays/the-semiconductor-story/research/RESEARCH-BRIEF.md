## Research Scope Boundaries

### In Scope
- Semiconductor physics fundamentals (accessible level)
- Transistor and IC invention history
- Key companies and founders
- Manufacturing process overview
- Geopolitical/supply chain dimensions
- AI compute revolution connection

### Out of Scope
- Detailed quantum mechanics
- Specific technical specifications of individual processors
- Financial/investment analysis
- Competitor analysis beyond historical context
- Future predictions beyond established trends
