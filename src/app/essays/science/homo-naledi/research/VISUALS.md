## Processing Pipeline

1. **Download** — Retrieve full-resolution figures from eLife (via DOI) and Wikimedia Commons
2. **Extract** — Crop individual panels from multi-panel eLife figures
3. **Normalize** — Scale-match specimens using published measurements; uniform backgrounds
4. **Composite** — Assemble comparative figures (IMG-23 through IMG-28) from constituent images
5. **Annotate** — Add scale bars, labels, and anatomical landmarks where needed
6. **Optimize** — Export at 2x display resolution for the essay; WebP + fallback JPEG
7. **Attribute** — Generate per-image attribution strings for figure captions
