### Image Processing Pipeline

1. Source at highest available resolution
2. Convert to WebP format (lossy, quality 85)
3. Generate responsive sizes: 640w, 1024w, 1600w, 2400w
4. Upload to Cloudflare R2 at `images.esy.com/essays/turkana-boy/`
5. Maintain attribution in `IMAGE_CREDITS` array in `images.ts`
6. All images served with `Cache-Control: public, max-age=31536000, immutable`

### License Priority

1. **Public Domain / CC0** — no attribution required, maximum flexibility
2. **CC BY / CC BY-SA** — attribution required, commercially usable
3. **Museum permission** — case-by-case, may restrict modification
4. **Academic fair use** — last resort, not suitable for commercial publication
5. **Custom illustration** — full control, preferred for comparative diagrams where no free source exists
