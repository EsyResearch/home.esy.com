**Verdict**: ✅ Motion language is pedagogical and subject-derived.

**Verdict**: ✅ Photography strategy matches the actual visual record of the subject.

### Collision Assessment

| Essay Context | Potential Overlap | Reconciliation |
|---------------|-------------------|----------------|
| Other dark Esy essays | dark background systems | Turkana Boy uses open-air framing, blue-green environmental accent, and exposed-distance logic rather than darkness-as-mood |

| Other paleoanthropology essays | bone / geology family resemblance | continuity is expected at the domain level, but Turkana Boy is differentiated by climate exposure, blue-green lake cue, and locomotion-first motifs |

### Unique Design Signals

- `Turkana Blue` as a real environmental accent tied to Lake Turkana rather than a decorative brand color
- ghost skeleton motif tied to completeness + developmental incompletion
- stride lines and distance markers as a core organizational pattern
- discovery imagery grounded in thorn tree / slope / field attention rather than cave or excavation-chamber drama
- forensic annotation as a primary aesthetic device

### Novelty Verdict

No direct aesthetic collision is identified. The design remains clearly within the Esy essay family, but its key signals are specific enough that it should not be mistaken for a generic dark science essay or a reuse of a previous paleoanthropology mood.

### Overall Cross-Essay Novelty: ✅ PASS
