## 3D / Interactive Notes

- African Fossils indicates photogrammetry/digital capture is available for at least the mandible entry for KNM-WT 15000.
- Smithsonian currently appears stronger as a specimen-summary source than as a direct 3D provider.
- A 3D viewer remains viable if we can secure a clean educational scan or derivative cast model; otherwise the fallback should be a high-resolution annotated specimen image.

## Visual Direction Notes

- This essay should feel sunlit, exposed, and open-air, not subterranean or cave-dark.
- The strongest visual tension is **bright landscape + clinical bone**.
- Specimen images should be paired with comparative silhouettes and clean scientific overlays rather than decorative textures.
