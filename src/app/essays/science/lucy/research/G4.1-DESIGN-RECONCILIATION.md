### Typography Authenticity
- Serif-forward justified by: deep-time science, archaeological authority, museum placard tradition
- Monospace for specimen IDs justified by: taxonomic register convention (AL 288-1, DIK-1-1)
- Sans-serif for data/anatomical terms justified by: clinical precision, visual distinction from narrative

**Verdict**: ✅ Typography choices trace to subject conventions.

### Animation Authenticity
- Bone assembly hero: directly represents fossil reconstruction process
- Gait animation (D10): biomechanical data visualization, not decorative
- Scroll-lock for skeleton tour: mirrors museum gallery walk-through experience
- Species time map: temporal data drives animation, not arbitrary motion

**Verdict**: ✅ All animation serves revelation, not decoration.
