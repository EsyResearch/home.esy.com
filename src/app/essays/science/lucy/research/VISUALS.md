## Sourcing Strategy

### Primary Archives
1. **Wikimedia Commons** — First stop for CC-licensed specimen photography
2. **Cleveland Museum of Natural History** — Holds high-quality Lucy casts; press office for image requests
3. **Ethiopian National Museum** — Original specimens; permission requests required
4. **National Museums of Kenya** — East African specimens
5. **Smithsonian Human Origins** — Excellent photography, some CC
6. **Institute of Human Origins (ASU)** — Discovery-era archival photos

### Licensing Priority
1. Public domain / CC0
2. CC-BY or CC-BY-SA
3. Editorial use (Getty/Alamy) — last resort for landscapes
4. Permission-based (museum direct) — for unique specimens
