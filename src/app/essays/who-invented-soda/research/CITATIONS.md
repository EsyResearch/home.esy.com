## Key Facts Supported

- Joseph Priestley's 1767 discovery of carbonated water
- Jacob Schweppe's 1783 commercial carbonation process
- John Pemberton's 1886 creation of Coca-Cola in Atlanta
- Caleb Bradham's 1893 creation of Pepsi-Cola ("Brad's Drink")
- 1929 introduction of coin-operated vending machines
- 1985 New Coke disaster and rapid reversal
- Global market statistics (400B+ market, 1.9B daily servings)
