# Visual Requirements: Rock & Roll Research Package

## Visual Treatment Philosophy

**Medium:** Photorealistic (archival photography only)
**No illustrations** except for technical diagrams (guitar schematics, studio layouts)
**Era-based processing** applied to all images

---

## Era Treatments

### 1920s-1940s: Deep History
- **Processing:** Deep sepia or desaturated warm tones
- **Grain:** Heavy, visible film grain
- **Contrast:** High contrast, crushed blacks
- **Feel:** Museum artifact, recovered history

### 1950s: The Explosion
- **Processing:** High contrast black & white (majority)
- **Color:** Where available, Kodachrome tones (warm yellows, reds)
- **Grain:** Medium, classic film look
- **Feel:** Documentary, photojournalistic

### 1960s: British Invasion & Beyond
- **Processing:** Saturated color, slightly faded
- **Style:** Documentary photography, concert shots
- **Feel:** Energy, movement, cultural moment

### 1970s-1990s: Evolution
- **Processing:** Period-appropriate film stocks
- **Style:** Mix of professional and vernacular photography
- **Feel:** Genre-specific (punk grit, hair metal sheen, grunge documentary)
