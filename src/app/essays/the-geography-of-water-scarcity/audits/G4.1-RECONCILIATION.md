### Non-Transferability Test

**Statement**: "This visual identity is unique because it is the first data journalism essay in the Esy catalogue... combining editorial serif typography with dark-canvas programmatic data visualizations... The color system derives from water and drought imagery... The particle flow animation (Sankey) is uniquely water-themed"

**Assessment**: ✅ **PASS** — This design could NOT work for another subject because:
1. The 5-class choropleth scale (blue → amber → red) derives from water stress specifically
2. Agriculture-green, industry-amber, domestic-teal are sector-specific to water allocation
3. The particle flow animation mimics water, not generic data
4. The "emergence from darkness" motif references invisible aquifer depletion
5. No other Esy essay uses programmatic data visualizations as primary medium

### Phase 1 Result: **95%** ✅ PASS

Minor deduction: `--bg-elevated` and `--bg-surface` are systematic elevation variants rather than directly traced to physical artifacts. This is standard practice and acceptable.
