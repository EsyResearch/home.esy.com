APA (American Psychological Association) format is the standard citation style for social sciences, psychology, education, and business papers.

## When to Use APA

- Psychology and behavioral sciences
- Education and nursing
- Business and management
- Social sciences

## Paper Format

- 1-inch margins on all sides
- Double-spaced text
- 12-point Times New Roman font
- Running head with page numbers
- Title page with author information

## In-Text Citations

Format: **(Author, Year, p. #)**

- One author: (Smith, 2020, p. 45)
- Two authors: (Smith & Johnson, 2020)
- Three+ authors: (Smith et al., 2020)

## Reference List Examples

**Journal Article:**
Smith, J. (2020). Title of article. *Journal Name*, *15*(2), 123-145. https://doi.org/10.1000/example

**Book:**
Johnson, M. (2019). *Book title*. Publisher Name.

**Website:**
Brown, L. (2023, January 15). Article title. *Website Name*. https://www.example.com/article

## Quick Tips

- Use "&" in parenthetical citations, "and" in narrative
- Include DOIs for journal articles when available
- Alphabetize references by author's last name
- Use hanging indent for reference entries
